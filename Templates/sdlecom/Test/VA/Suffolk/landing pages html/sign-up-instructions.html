Registration Instructions

Notice

For questions regarding your user account or the use of this site, please contact 866-793-6505 x 295.
There is a fee for accessing the images contained in the Clerk’s Office Land Records System. You may use the indexes without a fee. The method of access for images will be via a "Monthly Subscription". A complete explanation of this method may be accessed by clicking on "Subscriber Agreement" below. By law, all users must fill out and file an original of the "Registration Form", also available for download below. Every user must submit a registration form and subscriber agreement. We also require a background check on all users. The "Applicant Release and Authorization Form" below, must be completed and submitted with a $15.00 processing fee payable to Suffolk Circuit Court.

The data contained on this web site contains public information maintained by the Office of The Clerk of the Circuit Court for the City of Suffolk. This site has both general information (the indexes) and digital copies of the actual documents (images) for the following record types:
  • Deeds indexes from January 1974 to Present
  • Judgement indexes from July 1998 and images since January 2000
  • Plats indexes and images from 1974 to Present
  • Uniform Commercial Code Filings (UCC’s) from July 1998 and images since April 2006
  • Fiduciary filing indexes from April 1998 and images since January 2001
