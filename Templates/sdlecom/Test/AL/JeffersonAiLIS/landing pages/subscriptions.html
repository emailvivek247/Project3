Available Subscriptions

The available subscriptions for Jefferson County are listed below. To access these subscriptions, please Sign In or Register.

Unlimited Access with Images


  • Subscription cost: $100/month
  • Access to Jefferson County Probate Judge's public records.
  • Unlimited Image Access with Unofficial Watermark (Official copies are available at the additional cost of $2.00 per page, $3.00 minimum)
  • Includes Premium Features like Phonetic Search, etc.

Pay-As-You-Go Index Access


  • Subscription cost: $2 per image (minimum $3 per document)
  • Access to Jefferson County Probate Judge's public records.
  • Ability to view and print documents.
  • Does not include Premium Features.
